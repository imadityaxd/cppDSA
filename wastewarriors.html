The Problem: A Growing Crisis

  • Overwhelmed Systems

    Our cities face a massive waste crisis, and general awareness is low.

  • Ineffective Outreach

    Traditional awareness campaigns are failing to engage people meaningfully.

  • Lack of Tools

    Communities, especially students, lack interactive tools to learn sustainable practices.

Our Solution: Waste Warriors

An interactive, gamified platform for waste management education.

Players become "Waste Warriors" in a city simulation, managing recycling, composting, and awareness programs. The goal is to make sustainability fun, engaging, and rewarding!
